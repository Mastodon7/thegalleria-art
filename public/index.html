Artist Statement

Carolyn Elaine

Carolyn Elaine is a Chicago-based public artist whose work explores history, identity, and the stories that connect people to place. Working primarily in mosaic, she creates permanent installations that transform architecture into spaces of reflection, belonging, and cultural memory.

Through collaboration with communities, institutions, and design partners, her work celebrates the people, traditions, and histories that shape our shared experience.
